History of St. Antony’s Church, Kannamaly

The history of St. Antony’s Church, Kannamaly spans over 281 years. The first church was established in 1745 and was later rebuilt multiple times due to severe sea erosion.

The present church, modeled after St. Paul’s Basilica in Rome, was solemnly blessed on 22 November 1942 and is now revered under the patronage of St. Antony.
