Welcome

St. Antony’s Church, Kannamaly is a faith-filled parish with a sacred heritage spanning over 281 years. Rooted in devotion and shaped by resilience through history, the Church stands today as a living testimony of God’s grace and the loving intercession of St. Antony.

The Kannamaly St. Joseph Nercha Sadya, observed since 1905, commemorates a miraculous healing from a cholera outbreak and is held every year on 19th March.

The Miraculous Statue of St. Joseph of Kannamaly, brought from Spain in 1905, continues to draw thousands of devotees seeking his powerful intercession.
