St. Joseph Nercha Sadya

The sacred Nercha Sadya began in 1905 during a devastating cholera outbreak. Through the intercession of St. Joseph, the disease was miraculously brought under control.

Every year on March 19, lakhs of devotees participate in this holy offering with deep faith and gratitude.
